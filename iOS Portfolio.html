An iPhone app that draws multiple paths of different potential bike routes over a map of San Francisco. The elevation of the different route segments are shaded in different colors to represent how steep of a climb the hills are and, uniquely, the segments are colored by incline only in the direction of travel. By showing multiple routes at the same time, the hills are visible and your desired type of ride to your destination can be determined. Do you want to climb hills or ride around them? Now you can see what your route looks like before you go.
